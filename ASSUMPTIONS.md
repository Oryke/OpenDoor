# Assumptions

This document tracks the assumptions behind OpenDoor.

They're not facts.

They're ideas that need evidence.

---

## Assumption 1

Many beginners leave technology because of emotional and social barriers rather than technical ability alone.

**Status:** 🔬 Being researched

---

## Assumption 2

Existing communities don't fully support people between learning and contributing.

**Status:** 🔬 Being researched

---

## Assumption 3

Helping someone make their first meaningful contribution increases their confidence to continue.

**Status:** 🔬 Being researched

---

## Assumption 4

There is still room for a new approach despite the number of existing beginner communities.

**Status:** 🔬 Being researched

---

As research progresses, assumptions may be validated, revised, or completely removed.
